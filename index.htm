Welcome to Flower Bud Village Cafe and Bakery. Our bakery has been serving delicious pastries to Flower Bud Village for generations. Head Baker Jeff, and Elli, Ellen's granddaughter, continue this tradition and hope to see you and your family soon.

Harvest Festival

Join us for the Harvest Festival on October 12. Children of all ages are welcome. Flower Bud Village Bakery will be providing food and drink. If you find the coin in your piece of cake, you will be next year's Harvest King. Don't miss out on the chance to ride the hot air ballon during the Planting Festival. See you there!
